Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Integration AI LLC ("we", "us", "our") provides software solutions for property management communications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect phone numbers and names of individuals who receive SMS messages through our platform. This information is provided by our clients (property management companies) or directly by the individuals who opt in to receive messages.

How We Use Your Information

We use personal information solely to deliver SMS notifications on behalf of our clients. These notifications include account alerts, payment reminders, maintenance updates, and service-related messages. We do not use personal information for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes. Messages are transmitted through Twilio, our messaging service provider, solely for the purpose of delivering SMS notifications.

Data Protection

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at team.integrationai@gmail.com.
